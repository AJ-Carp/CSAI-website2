About CSAI

We’re a student-run group exploring AI and computer science through hands-on builds, talks, and mentorship. Whether you’re new or deep into research, you’ll find collaborators, guidance, and a stage to showcase your work.

Hands-on

Build real systems: agentic workflows, vision pipelines, simulation tooling, and research-grade prototypes that become portfolio pieces.

Community

Weekly meetups, open labs, and hack nights. Pair up with mentors and teammates who accelerate your learning curve.

Showcase

Demo days, cross-campus collabs, and blog spotlights. Publish write-ups, ship public repos, and present to partners.
